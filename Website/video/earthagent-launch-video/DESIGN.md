## Style Prompt

Earth Agent uses a premium Mac startup visual identity: quiet graphite surfaces, glassy panels, luminous Earth blues and greens, precise typography, and restrained cinematic motion. The mood is intelligent, safe, and calm rather than noisy or cyberpunk.

## Colors

- Background: `#07100f`, deep green-black graphite
- Surface: `#101a19`, soft elevated panel
- Surface glass: `rgba(214, 244, 236, 0.10)`
- Foreground: `#eef8f4`, warm white
- Muted text: `#a8bbb5`
- Accent: `#4fe7b0`, Earth signal green
- Secondary accent: `#75b8ff`, orbital blue
- Caution: `#f8c96b`, confirmation highlight

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, `SF Pro Display`, `system-ui`, sans-serif
- Technical labels: `SF Mono`, `JetBrains Mono`, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- No purple-blue gradient-dominant theme.
- No cluttered dashboard grids.
- No stock photos or fake app store badge.
- No tiny unreadable UI text.
- No overclaiming full autonomous control.
